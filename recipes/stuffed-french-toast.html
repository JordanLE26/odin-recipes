Stuffed French Toast

Description

This fluffy stuffed French toast makes a lovely change from ordinary French toast. This recipe is my family's favorite version, but it is very easy to adapt to use your favorite flavor of jam or peanut butter — the options are practically limitless! Serve with maple syrup and sprinkle with confectioners' sugar.

Ingredients

  • 8 slices oatnut bread
  • 1/2 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup seedless raspberry jam
  • 5 eggs, beaten
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons almond extract
  • 1 tablespoon butter

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Spread softened cream cheese on 4 bread slices; spread raspberry jam on remaining 4 bread slices.
  3. Press cream cheese and jam bread slices together to form 4 sandwiches.
  4. Beat eggs, milk, and almond extract together in a shallow bowl.
  5. Melt butter in a large, heavy skillet over medium-high heat. Dip sandwiches in egg mixture to coat evenly.
  6. Cook sandwiches on both sides until golden brown, 2 to 4 minutes per side.
  7. Enjoy!
